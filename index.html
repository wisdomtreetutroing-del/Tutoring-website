Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our tutoring services

What subjects do you offer tutoring in?
+

We offer comprehensive tutoring across all major subjects including Maths, English, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), and Functional Skills. We also provide support in other subjects upon request.

What are your tutoring rates?
+

Our rates vary depending on the level of study, subject, and whether you choose one-to-one or group sessions. Please contact us for a personalized quote tailored to your needs.

Do you offer online tutoring?
+

Yes! We offer both in-person and online tutoring sessions. Our online lessons use interactive platforms that make learning engaging and effective from the comfort of your home.

How do I book a session?
+

Simply fill out the contact form below or give us a call. We'll discuss your requirements, match you with the perfect tutor, and schedule your first session at a time that works for you.

What qualifications do your tutors have?
+

All our tutors are qualified professionals with relevant degrees and teaching certifications. They undergo thorough background checks and have proven track records of helping students achieve their academic goals.
